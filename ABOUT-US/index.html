ABOUT US

Work Profile

Our organization procures machinery under strict supervision of senior engineers. The machines undergo a strict visual & mechanical inspection by our technical team. The machine is again inspected in our workshop and on satisfaction it is moved up for overhaul where the machinery gets ready with all marine standards and ensuring it to work on vessel efficiently without a hitch.

QUALITY POLICY

Best Supply

We supply good quality machinery and spares. The old machinery and spares supplied shall have sufficient life and we make it in performance close to new. We supply best possible service as per professional standards in marine surveys.

Visual & Mechanical Inspection

We shall conduct regular internal audits and any failure to comply with the QMS or intent of the top management shall be reviewed and used as positive indicators for improvement.

Satisfaction

We undertake to communicate this policy to members of the organization and ensure that it is carried out with a clear understanding of the management’s intent to pursue a policy of continual improvement of all aspects of our business.
